Your Perfect Day Starts Here

The Wisconsin State Fair is full of tasty treats, exhilarating rides and packed full of Wisconsin agriculture. There is a lot to see over the 200-acre park, but these must-visit stops will make your trip to the state fair one to remember.

Explore Fair Highlights

Start in the Barns

Visit the livestock barns early to see dairy cattle, sheep, and swine up close. Agriculture is at the heart of the fair.

Taste the Tradition

Try classic Wisconsin favorites like cheese curds and cream puffs while exploring food stands throughout the grounds.

Stay for the Night Lights

Enjoy live music, rides, and entertainment as the sun sets over the fairgrounds.

Fair Day Tips

  • Arrive early to avoid crowds
  • Wear comfortable shoes
  • Bring water and sunscreen
  • Visit the barns before heading to rides

Review the official schedule for livestock shows, concerts, and special events before you go.
